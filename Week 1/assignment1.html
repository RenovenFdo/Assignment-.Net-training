Ojai Trails

Northridge Loop


posted on January 17th,2014

TRAIL INFO

trail type single track
elevation: 2000 ft
length: 14.5 miles
skill level: beginner to intermediate

If you are looking for a great scenery and a great workout without all the technical difficulty of other trails.Northridge loop is for you.The ridge runs east to west, with Ojai Valley on one side and Rose Valley on the other.The trail features amazing views of both valleysplus theLos Padres National forest.

There are a total of 6 trail beads spread and over the 14 mile loop,so there are plenty of place to cool off from the exhausting and awesome experience in the hills
